Scoring Criteria

# Criterion Scoring Guide (1–5) Weight Score (1–5) Weighted
1 Audience Alignment
Are Tamara's primary audiences (investors, regulators, merchants, media) present and reachable? 		1=Wrong audience · 3=Partial overlap · 5=All primary audiences present ×1.0 —
2 Narrative Fit
Does the event's theme and reputation align with Tamara's current strategic narrative? 		1=No fit · 3=Adjacent · 5=Perfect category match ×1.0 —
3 Geographic + Market Relevance
Is this event in a market where Tamara is active or planning to enter? 		1=Irrelevant market · 3=Adjacent market · 5=Core market (KSA/UAE) ×0.75 —
4 Announcement / Milestone Opportunity
Is there a major Tamara announcement or milestone that can be amplified at this event? 		1=No announcement · 3=Minor news · 5=Major milestone (funding, license, product launch) ×1.0 —
5 Competitive Landscape
What is Tamara's positioning relative to competitors at this event? 		1=Competitors dominate · 3=Level playing field · 5=Home advantage / only regional leader ×0.75 —
6 ROI Measurability
Can we set clear, measurable targets and track them before/during/after the event? 		1=Unmeasurable · 3=Partial metrics available · 5=Full ROI framework applicable ×0.75 —
7 Operational Feasibility
Given current team capacity and budget, can Tamara execute this event at the required standard? 		1=Team/budget inadequate · 3=Feasible with stretching · 5=Fully resourced and ready ×0.75 —
