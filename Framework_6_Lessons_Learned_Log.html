90-MINUTE FACILITATION AGENDA
0:00–0:10
Context setting (PMO)
Share ROI Dashboard results. Set ground rules: no blame, focus on system not people. Every issue has a root cause, every root cause has a recommendation.
0:10–0:55
Category walkthrough (all)
Work through all 12 categories in order. 3–4 minutes per category. Log what worked + what to improve. Assign owner to every recommendation before moving on.
0:55–1:10
Priority ranking (group)
Identify top 3 high-impact improvements that must be applied to the next event. Mark as priority in log. Confirm owners and deadlines.
1:10–1:25
Framework update decisions (PMO)
Which of the 6 frameworks needs updating based on this session? Agree edits. PMO updates within 5 business days.
1:25–1:30
Close (PMO)
Confirm: all entries logged, all recommendations owned, next review date (W12 of next event). Share log with all attendees within 24hrs.
