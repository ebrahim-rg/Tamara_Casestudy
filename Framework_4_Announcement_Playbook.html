TAMARA COMMUNICATIONS PMO — FRAMEWORK 4 OF 6

Announcement Playbook

End-to-end execution guide for major announcements at sponsored events. Five phases · Checklist format · Adapted for Money 20/20 ME 2025.

How to use: Work through all five phases in sequence. Items marked CRITICAL are non-negotiable — do not proceed to the next phase without these completed. The 15-Minute Day-Of Window is the operational core of this playbook: every team member must be briefed on their exact action at least 24hrs before D1. Owner tags indicate who is responsible.
