PMO OPERATING PRINCIPLES
Four rules that govern how this project runs — built for a startup comms function, not a corporate PMO textbook
01
NARRATIVE BEFORE EXECUTION
No workstream opens until the story is locked. A booth built around the wrong narrative is a $380K mistake.
Booth brief waits for CMO narrative sign-off. Not the other way around.
02
DEPENDENCY-FIRST PLANNING
I map what cannot start until something else is done — before building the timeline. Critical path identified in Week 1, not Week 8.
Design assets cannot go to Tahaluf until legal clears the announcement narrative.
03
SPEED THROUGH CLARITY
Every person on this project knows their lane, their deadline, and their escalation path before they start. Ambiguity is the enemy of speed.
No "who's handling the PR agency brief?" conversations in Week 4. That's in the RACI, agreed in Week 12.
04
BUILD ONCE, SCALE ALWAYS
Every template, tracker, and process created for this event becomes Tamara's permanent sponsorship infrastructure.
The Announcement Playbook built for Money 20/20 runs LEAP, GITEX, and every future event.
